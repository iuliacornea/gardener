What it is

A potted plant with it's own, private gardener. The One pot gardener, as the name says is an semi-automated gardener for one pot.

What it does

It automatically waters your plant per its specific needs. On top of that, it will also tell you if your plant has enough light, is in an appropriately dry setting and it's surrounding temperature is not too cold or too hot.

How it does it

With some sensors that monitor your plant's environment, a microcontroller, a water pump and (this is where you come in) a water supply that you need to provide for the gardener, it will take good care of your plant no matter if you are on vacation or not.

How it looks like

Here is the best part! It can look however you want. Whichever shape, color, texture or size. After you fill in our form to get your one pot gardener, somebody from our team will get in contact with you and you will discuss this together. Choosing the best plant for you and your apartment and making sure it stays alive, has never been easier.
