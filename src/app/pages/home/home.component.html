One pot gardener

John Muir said "The clearest way into the Universe is through a forest wilderness." But what if you don't have a forest close by? Well, then it might be a garden. But in the case the garden is out of reach, how about a potted plant? A potted plant that will take care of you more than you have to take care of it. The secret: it comes with its own gardener.

Fred

Fred has been taken cared by our gardener since one month now. He needed more light and, after that, his teenager growth period started.

Maya and Leia

These lovely ladies were a bit too hot for their own sake. Since our gardener noticed the temperature, they are now enjoying a cooler environment, further away from the radiator.

Mosty

Mosty survived just fine on his own, but with his own private gardener, and the awareness of his water needs (a much larger supply), he now thrives.

Our mission

Our purpose is simple, our mission precise: survival of your plant. Do not worry if you don't have green thumbs, leave for your vacation carefree, our gardeners will take care of it for you.
